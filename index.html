Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Grid ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). Grid is a technology platform that facilitates communication and advisory services between clients and SEBI-registered Investment Advisers.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you register and use the App:

  • Personal Information: Full name, email address, phone number, city of residence
  • Identity Information: PAN card number (required for SEBI compliance)
  • Financial Information: Annual income, total assets, total liabilities, monthly expenses (for risk profiling purposes)
  • Usage Data: Session history, messages exchanged with advisors, advice reports
  • Audio Recordings: Call recordings between clients and advisors (with consent of both parties, retained for SEBI-mandated 5-year record-keeping)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, push notification tokens

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To connect you with SEBI-registered Investment Advisers
  • To generate your Investment Policy Statement (IPS) and suitability assessments
  • To process advisory fees and payments via Razorpay
  • To send transactional emails (account verification, password reset) via Resend
  • To comply with SEBI (Investment Advisers) Regulations, 2013
  • To maintain 5-year mandatory record retention as required by SEBI
  • To send push notifications related to your sessions and account

3. Information Shared With Advisors

When you initiate a session with a SEBI-registered Investment Adviser and sign an advisory agreement, your profile information (name, email, risk profile, financial summary) is shared with that adviser to enable them to provide personalised financial advice. This sharing occurs only with your explicit consent as part of the advisory agreement process.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the App:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, cloud functions, and push notifications
  • Razorpay: Payment processing for advisory fees
  • Resend: Transactional email delivery
  • Google Sign-In: Optional OAuth-based authentication

Each of these services has their own privacy policies governing their use of your data.

5. Data Security

All data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. Data stored in Firebase is protected by Firebase Security Rules ensuring only authenticated users can access their own data. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

6. Audio Recording

With the explicit consent of both the client and the adviser, advisory calls may be recorded for compliance and record-keeping purposes as mandated by SEBI regulations. Recordings are stored securely and retained for the mandatory 5-year period. They are not used for any purpose other than regulatory compliance and dispute resolution.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. Advisory records, session transcripts, and call recordings are retained for a minimum of 5 years as required by SEBI (Investment Advisers) Regulations, 2013. Upon account deletion, personal data not subject to regulatory retention requirements will be deleted within 30 days.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data (subject to regulatory retention obligations)
  • Withdraw consent for optional data processing

To request account deletion, visit: https://forms.gle/R8pfC98ZLKw5JW3g9

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy in the App and updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
